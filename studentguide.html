Check out a cool event

Gainesville has lots of events happening year-round, which you can find on the City Events Calendar.





Bo Diddley Plaza, Downtown Gainesville



Bo Diddley is Gainesville's downtown community plaza, used as a event space and music venue. Check out their upcoming events such as free yoga.



Farmer's Market Wednesdays

Come join the community at Gainesville's downtown farmer's market on Wednesdays from 4-7pm for local food & veggies, live music, and craft vendors. People love to hang out and have picnics, play with their kids, and even do some hula hooping and acrobatics! Find out more here.



Free Fridays

From May through October you can hang out on Friday nights from 8-10pm and listen to music at Bo Diddley Plaza. There are always different genres and performers, check the schedule of upcoming shows.



Jobs & Internships- City of Gainesville



Jobs



Find a job with the City of Gainesville here. You can find positions ranging from part-time swim instructors and recreation assistants to full-time engineers.







Internships



The City of Gainesville Parks, Recreation and Cultural Affairs Department (PRCA) offers internships you can find out about here.

As a UF student, you can apply to the City of Gainesville Local Government Fellowship through the Bob Graham Center.

Be sure to check search engines such as Chegg's www.internships.com and www.indeed.com (or others) for internships with the city or organizations around Gainesville!
